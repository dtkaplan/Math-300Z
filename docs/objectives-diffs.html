Lesson 19

Stat 300 Lesson 19

  1. Explain the need for randomization in sampling.

  2. Explain the impact of sample size on the sample distribution and number of replications on the sampling procedure.

  3. Using a sampling distribution, make decisions about the population. In other words, understanding the effect of sampling variation on our estimates.

Stat 300Z Lesson 19

  1. Use and explain the terms accuracy and precision and the technical terms bias and variance.

  2. Carry out (using software) a multiple regression and interpret the coefficients as marginal effects.

  3. Demonstrate sampling variation using sub-sampling on simple and multiple-regression model coefficients.
